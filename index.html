About_Us

Welcome to Bhagirathi Travels

We, a team of professionally qualified Himalayan people feel pleasure to introduce ourselves with the name of "Bhagirathi Travels" founded in 1995 by Mr. SS Panwar. We are one of the leading Mussoorie & Dehradun based travel agency offering a wide array of travel services to the Individual tourist, families & groups in northern India specially in Uattrakhand. We organize various tours to CharDham Yatra, Garhwal Himalayas, Kumaun Hills, Transport For group & Business package.

Contact us for any type of facilities regarding package tour's for Individual, Group or Business Travel. We have a Young fleet all types of Latest Taxies, Cabs like Honda-City, Ford-Ikon, Swift-Dzire, Ambassador, Indigo, Indica, Innova, Tempo Traveller etc. for all over India. We follow strict quality control and superior service standards to make your journey one of its kind. We believe that, what you need is one who understands your requirements, a specialist who knows the destinations.
We also work's with various Government & Private organizations here in Uttarakhand.
